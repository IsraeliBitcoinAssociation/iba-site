Welcome to the
Israeli Bitcoin Association

The Israeli Bitcoin Association is here to promote Bitcoin usage across Israeli businesses and consumers and provide legal tools to integrate Bitcoin as a legitemate payment method.

Our Members

The Association in Israel was established by Ron Gross, Meni Rosenfeld, Eli Sklar, Eden Shochat, and Jonathan Ruach who pioneered Bitcoin usage across Israel and established local communities that support and use Bitcoin.
